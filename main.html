Summary

Inception is a sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state. Cobb is offered a chance to erase his criminal past by planting an idea into someone's mind—a process known as inception.
